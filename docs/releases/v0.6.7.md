# RiffNotes v0.6.7

This release focuses on fingerprint review ergonomics and faster teaching from the take list.

## Highlights

- Teach a fingerprint correction from the take row and remove the current guess after it is saved.
- Make quick remembered-title selection also teach the fingerprint system, so renaming a take clears the guess immediately.
- Increase spacing and header prominence in Preferences so settings groups are easier to distinguish.
- Keep the take row showing the highest raw fingerprint guess with confidence-colored text.
